About us

A boutique built on generosity

Fram Prom Boutique is a sustainability-led initiative at Framingham Earl High School. We collect gently used formalwear donated by the school community, giving clothes a second life and keeping them out of landfill.


Whether it's a stunning dress, a sharp suit, or the finishing accessories, every item in our boutique has been given with care and love by someone in our community. Items are available in exchange for a voluntary donation — whatever you can give is gratefully received.

Our aim

Making prom accessible for everyone

We set out to make prom more sustainable and more inclusive — reducing textile waste while supporting students across our community to access formalwear they'd otherwise struggle to afford. Each year group that donates leaves a lasting legacy for the students who come after them.


In our first year we are aiming to collect 50+ prom outfits, save dozens of garments from waste, help make prom more affordable for local families, and support a chosen charity.

How to donate

Pass it forward in four simple steps

Donating takes minutes. All we ask is that items are clean and in good condition.

  • 1

    Gather your items

    Dresses, suits, shirts, ties, shoes, jewellery, bags — anything prom-related is welcome.

  • 2

    Make sure they're clean

    Wash or dry-clean items before donating so they're ready to wear straight away.

  • 3

    Bring it into school

    Drop items off at school reception or with a designated member of staff. No need to arrange anything in advance.

  • 4

    Complete your donation tag

    You will be asked to fill in a small tag to attach to your item. This includes details such as the size, brand, age of the item, and any signs of wear or damage. You can add your name if you'd like to, but this is optional. A copy of the tag is shown below — you're welcome to print and complete one before you arrive.

    Donation Tag · Fram Prom Boutique

    Item type_______________
    Size_______________
    Brand_______________
    Approx. age_______________
    Condition / notes_______________
    Your name (optional)_______________

    Thank you for your donation ♡

  • 5

    We do the rest

    Items are sorted, steamed if needed, and added to the boutique ready for students to choose from in person.
